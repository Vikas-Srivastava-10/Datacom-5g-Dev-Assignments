The Role of RRC in 5G

The Radio Resource Control (RRC) protocol exists in Layer 3 of the 5G NR control plane protocol stack. It acts as the "Controller" that configures all lower layers to ensure a reliable radio link.

  • Configuration: Defines how the UE and gNB communicate at the physical level.
  • QoS Mapping: Ensures that different types of traffic (Vo5G, Video, Web) get the correct priority.
  • UE Power Saving: Controls DRX (Discontinuous Reception) cycles.

RRC State Transitions

The 5G RRC introduces the Inactive State to reduce signaling overhead for small data bursts while keeping the battery life long.

  • IDLE to CONNECTED: Triggered by User Data or Paging.
  • CONNECTED to INACTIVE: Triggered by inactivity timer to save power.
  • INACTIVE to CONNECTED: Resume procedure using stored UE context.
