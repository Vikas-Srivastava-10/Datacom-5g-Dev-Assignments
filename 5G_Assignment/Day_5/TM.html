RLC TM Overview
Transparent Mode (TM) is the simplest RLC mode.

Characteristics:
  • No RLC Header added (0 overhead).
  • No Segmentation or Reassembly.
  • No ARQ (No ACK/NACK).
Use Cases: Broadcast Control Channel (BCCH), Paging (PCCH), and Common Control (CCCH/SRB0).
